MoonConf Sponsorship Prospectus

What is MoonConf?

MoonConf is a diverse three-day developer conference, created as a celebration of functional programming and community.

To ensure the conference is accessible to anyone who chooses to attend, conference tickets will be inexpensive, with plenty of scholarships available. Your support will ensure that MOONCONF is a success.

Where: Phoenix, AZ (to be confirmed)

When: November 2017

Estimated Attendance: 220

Why should you consider sponsoring MoonConf?

The audience for MOONCONF will be comprised of a diverse amalgam of software engineers, industry professionals, and students of various levels. We want to showcase some of the most motivated work within the functional programming community, while also providing introductory sessions geared toward beginner and intermediate level functional programmers.

This year’s inaugural conference will feature a full day of curated talks followed by a full day unconference.

We know you’ll love this event, we love it too; our organizers are flexible and motivated to find the best approach possible in order to maximize the value of your sponsorship.

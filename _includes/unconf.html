un·con·fer·ence /ˈənkänf(ə)rəns/

Dive Deeper

After two days of talks that get your brains buzzing, what better than to have a chance to explore the topics more deeply?

Day 3 will be a full Open Space. We'll begin with a facilitated opening session, in which we decide together how we will spend or time.

The rest of the day will be full of pairing sessions, lighting talks, hackathons, whatever you need to get the most out of what you're learning.

Beginner Workshops

Parallel with the openspace, day 3 will also include beginner workshops. Anyone with coding experience is welcome.
